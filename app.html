Case Study Summary: Campus Jobs Mobile Application Development

OBJECTIVE

  • The Campus Jobs mobile application aims to facilitate Conestoga College students in accessing and applying for on-campus job opportunities efficiently. The goal is to create a user-friendly platform that keeps students updated on available positions, allowing them to sign up, apply, and schedule interviews seamlessly. The application benefits both students and college departments by streamlining the hiring process.
    • >

KEY FEATURES

  • Student Interaction:
  • After logging in, students can view job postings related to on-campus positions.
  • A simple and intuitive signup process using student ID.
  • Easy navigation to explore and apply for different job postings, including part-time and full-time opportunities.
  • Department Coordinator Dashboard:
  • Department coordinators have a dedicated login to view submissions from students seeking on-campus jobs.
  • Efficiently manage and review job applications submitted by students.

DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

  • Wireframes: The wireframes illustrate a clean and user-friendly interface with pages for login, job postings, job descriptions, and application submissions.
  • Functional Documentation: Detailed documentation outlines the functionality of each page, including login, welcome, home, job postings, job descriptions, and application submission.

USER STORIES

  • Student User Story:
  • After logging in, students can seamlessly navigate to the home page and explore job postings.
  • Students have the option to view job details, apply for positions, and schedule interviews through the application.
  • Department Coordinator User Story:
  • Department coordinators can log in separately to review and manage job applications submitted by students.

NEXT STEPS

  • The project involves further development and implementation of the designed features, ensuring a smooth and efficient application process for both students and department coordinators.

CONCLUSION

  • The Campus Jobs mobile application serves as a bridge between students and on-campus job opportunities, enhancing accessibility and convenience for all users involved in the hiring process.

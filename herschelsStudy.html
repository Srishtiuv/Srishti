Case Study Summary: Herschel's Social Media Strategy with Hootsuite

BACKGROUND

  • Herschel Supply Co., established in 2009, is a Canadian accessories brand known for retro backpacks and eco-friendly products.
  • Headquarters in Vancouver, British Columbia, with a global presence through 44 retail locations.

SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING GOALS

  • Utilize Hootsuite to expand online presence and manage social media channels effectively.
  • Enhance brand awareness, engagement, and eco-friendly product promotion through storytelling.

TARGET AUDIENCE

  • Primarily hipsters, school-aged children, and teenagers.
  • Emphasis on eco-conscious individuals.

KEY STRATEGIES

  • Social Media Channels:
  • Leverage Facebook (1M+ fans) and Instagram (1.1M followers) for brand storytelling and engagement.
  • Utilize Twitter for real-time updates and customer interaction.
  • Collaborations:
  • Partner with influencers like Marianne Theodorsen and eco-conscious travel blogger Valeria for product promotion.
  • Collaborate with major brands to increase visibility.
  • Engagement Tactics:
  • Encourage user-generated content with creative hashtags.
  • Offer gifts for engaging posts to boost online community participation.
  • Hootsuite Features:
  • Best Time to Publish: Optimize posting schedules for maximum impact.
  • Analytics: Track performance metrics across platforms for comprehensive insights.
  • Hootsuite Impact: Measure social ROI and track conversion rates.

SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN

  • Introduce the "We've Got Your Back" initiative, sending bags to healthcare facilities and supporting schools.
  • Collaborate with Hootsuite to analyze sentiment, responding to negative feedback and promoting positive interactions.

EXPANSION STRATEGIES

  • Influencers:
  • Collaborate with influencers with over 100K followers, training them on eco-friendly products.
  • Collaborations:
  • Engage in collaborations with major brands to increase product visibility and reach.
  • User Engagement:
  • Encourage audience engagement with creative hashtags and incentivized posts.

CUSTOMER INSIGHTS AND SENTIMENT AALYSIS

  • Use Hootsuite analytics to gain customer insights and perform sentiment analysis.
  • Respond thoughtfully to feedback, ensuring appropriate replies and maintaining brand values.

BUSINESS GAME PLAN

  • Stakeholders: Hootsuite responsible for implementation and sentiment analysis reporting.
  • Goals & Metrics: Track engagement, impressions, and sentiment metrics for continuous improvement.

JOB POSTING - IT SPECIALIST

  • Full-time permanent role to support Herschel's IT needs.
  • Responsibilities include back-end reporting, hardware/software support, and collaboration on projects.
  • Requirements: IT-related diploma/degree, one year of related experience, strong interpersonal and communication skills.

CONCLUSION

  • Herschel's collaboration with Hootsuite provides a comprehensive social media strategy, leveraging influencers, collaborations, and user engagement to enhance brand visibility and promote eco-friendly products. The utilization of Hootsuite's features ensures effective management and analysis of social media channels, contributing to Herschel's overall growth and success.

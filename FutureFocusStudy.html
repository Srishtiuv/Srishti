Case Study Summary: Future Focus Inc.

OBJECTIVE

  • Address pain points related to contact synchronization, CRM modules, workflows, digital marketing opportunities, and client relationship management in Zoho for Future Focus Inc.

PAIN POINTS

  • Seamless Contacts Integration (Outlook & Zoho).
  • CRM Modules and Workflow Setup.
  • Client Relationship and Alumni Forum Management.

SOLUTION

  • CardDAV Account/Platform Plugins:
  • Open internet protocol for syncing contacts.
  • Utilize CRM platforms for advanced tasks.
  • Contact Segmentation:
  • Improve engagement, retention, and ROI through personalized campaigns.
  • CRM Setup
  • Re-plan system requirements.
  • Choose/set up an appropriate CRM plan.
  • Consider alternate: HubSpot - comprehensive CRM platform.
  • Implementation Strategy & Plan:
  • Schedule process, planning, task assignment, implementation, testing, and maintenance.
  • Zoho Setup: 2 months.
  • HubSpot Setup: 2 months, 2 days.
  • Pricing Comparison (Zoho vs. HubSpot):
  • Zoho CRM Plus: $50/user/month (starting).
  • HubSpot Starter CRM Suite: $50/month (free for Lite users).
  • Gap Analysis:
  • Desired state: Unified CRM solution.
  • Current state: MVP at 80%, considering Zoho.
  • Gap: Evaluate whether Zoho meets requirements, and consider HubSpot as an alternate.

CONCLUSION

  • Zoho setup is an MVP at 80%, aiming for a unified solution.
  • An alternate solution is ready, considering HubSpot as a comprehensive CRM platform.

KEY POINTS

  • Comprehensive solution addressing pain points.
  • Strategic implementation plan for Zoho and HubSpot.
  • Cost-effective Lite user option in HubSpot.
  • Ongoing synchronization and maintenance processes.
  • Future-ready with an alternate solution.
